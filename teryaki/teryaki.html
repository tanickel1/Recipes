Steak Teryaki

This steak recipie is a great go to during grilling season. You can marinade the streaks in any sauce you want but we love Kikoman Green Onion and Garlic Teriyaki sauce.

Ingredients

  • Thin cut ribeye
  • plain white rice
  • Veggies. This goes good with edemame, but green beans work good as well.
  • Sauce

  1. Take the thinly sliced streak, and marinade in the sauce of choice, in the fridge, in a ziplock or bowl.
  2. While the streak marinades, start your rice, since that takes a long time generally in a rice cooker, or on a stove top.
  3. When the timer gets to the last few mintes on the rice cooker, start your microwave veggies, and throw your meat on the grill at a high temp, for 3-5 minutes each side, until achieve the proper burn marks, or meat temps.
  4. Throw the rice in a bowl, and top with veggies, and steak, and sprinkle a little extra sauce to taste.
