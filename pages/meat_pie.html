Meat Pie

This is a family favoriate for cold weather. We will make this recipe, and have enough leftovers for several days.

Ingredients

  • 2lbs Ground Meat
  • 1 cup chopped Carrot
  • 1 cup chopped Celery
  • 1/2 Cup Chopped Red Onion
  • 2 tbsp Minced Garlic
  • Tomato Paste
  • Worcester Sauce to taste
  • Broth
  • 1 Tbsp Flour
  • Seasonings
  • Instant Mashed Potatos

Steps

  1. Saute all veggies in Cast Iron Skillet
  2. Then Brown Meat
  3. Throw in Tomato Paste, Broth, Worcester Sauce to Taste, garlic, and flour
  4. Top with Instant Mashed Potatoes
  5. Broil till top is brown.
