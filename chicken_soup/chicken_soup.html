Chicken Noodle Sout

This is a recipie we have used for year whnever we are sick or it is cold out. The trick is being sure to use Egg Noodles and not wheat noodles, and being sure to saute the veggies before you start adding broth or meat.

Ingredients

  • Two Pounds Chicken Breast
  • One cup chopped baby Carrots
  • Once cup chopped celery
  • 8oz. Dry Egg Noodles
  • 1/2 Cup Chopped Red Onion
  • 8 cups Bone Broth
  • Seasonings to Taste

Steps

  1. Saute all veggies in the pan.
  2. Add broth at a low tempature and allow it to simmer thirty minutes.
  3. Add Chicken and Let continue to simmer another hour or so, until chicken pulls appart easily.
  4. Shred Chicken, then add Egg Noodles
  5. Add any seasonings, which can include, a little yellow curry, paprika, bay leaves, rosemary, salt, pepper and garlic powder.
  6. Let simmer with seasonings an additional 30 min at the lowest tempature
